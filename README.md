# Donate to Gaza

After years of blockade, more than 1.8 million Palestinians in Gaza are undergoing dense artillery shelling and
air raids by the Israeli military. In light of this escalation in violence and the resulting humanitarian crisis
and in the absence of escape from the carnage the sieged people of Gaza seek refuge in UNRWA schools which are
packed with tens of thousands who are in desperate need of shelter.

You can help in aiding the people of Gaza by donating to the humanitarian agencies supporting them and that work
on providing them with medical assistence, home repairs, food and shelter.
